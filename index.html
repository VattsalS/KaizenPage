Who We Are

We are a Mumbai-based creative agency obsessed with making brands impossible to ignore.

Founded on the Japanese philosophy of Kaizen — the art of continuous, incremental improvement — we apply the same discipline to everything we build. We don't do one-size-fits-all. We do strategy-first, story-led work that actually converts.

Our team is a collective of strategists, creatives, and digital specialists who've helped startups scale from zero and helped legacy brands rediscover their edge. We work as an extension of your team, not just a vendor you email twice a month.

What size businesses do you work with? +
We work with everyone from ambitious startups to established mid-market brands. Our sweet spot is companies who are serious about growth and ready to invest in marketing as a core business driver — not just a checkbox. If you have a product worth marketing, we'd love to talk.
Do you offer project-based work or retainers? +
Both. For clients who need ongoing brand building and campaign management, our monthly retainer packages make the most sense. For specific deliverables — a new brand identity, a website, a product launch campaign — we offer project-based engagements. We'll recommend the right structure after understanding your goals.
How long before we see real results? +
Paid campaigns can show traction within 2–4 weeks once optimised. SEO and organic content typically compound over 3–6 months. Brand work is long-term by nature. We're upfront about timelines from the start, and we share clear metrics so you always know what's moving and why.
What does onboarding look like? +
We start with a discovery call to understand your brand, goals, and market. From there we build a strategic brief, align on KPIs, and set up reporting. Most clients are live with their first campaigns within 2–3 weeks of signing. We keep the process lean and communication clear throughout.
Do you work with clients outside Mumbai? +
Absolutely. While we're rooted in Mumbai, our client base spans across India and internationally. We operate fully remotely with structured check-ins, shared dashboards, and async communication tools that make location irrelevant to the quality of work we deliver.
How do you measure success? +
Against goals we define together before starting. Every engagement begins with setting clear, measurable KPIs — whether that's leads, ROAS, follower growth, traffic, or conversions. You get monthly reports with honest commentary, not vanity metrics designed to obscure what's actually happening.
